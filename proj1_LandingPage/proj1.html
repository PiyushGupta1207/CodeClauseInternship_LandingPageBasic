Features

Frame Design

Durable and Lightweight Materials: Often made from aluminum, carbon fiber, or steel for strength and lightness. Geometry: Designed for stability and control on rough terrain with a sloped top tube and longer wheelbase.

Suspension System

Front Suspension (Hardtail): Absorbs shocks and bumps, providing a smoother ride. Full Suspension: Both front and rear suspension for maximum shock absorption and comfort.

Braking System

Disc Brakes:Hydraulics or mechanical, providing superior stopping power and control, especially in wet conditions. V-Brakes:Sometimes found on entry-level models for effective and simple braking.
