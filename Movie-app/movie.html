Interstellar

"Interstellar" (2014), directed by Christopher Nolan, is a science fiction epic that explores themes of love, sacrifice, and the survival of humanity. The film is set in a near-future Earth facing 
ecological collapse and follows a team of astronauts led by Cooper (Matthew McConaughey) as they embark on a journey through a wormhole near Saturn to find a new habitable planet.           
As they traverse through space and time, the team encounters various challenges, including extreme time dilation and uncharted celestial phenomena. The story delves into the nature of time, the power
of human connections, and the relentless pursuit of survival.
